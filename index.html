In my work I combine psychological theory with the latest design trends and web development standards to create engaging digital experiences for my clients and their end-users.

About me

I studied Psychology in Malta (B.Psy hons) and specialised in Organisational Psychology at Nottingham in the UK (MSc). Before starting my journey in web development I worked in human resources and educational psychology. There I have refined my organisational, analytical and problem-solving abilities and gained a better understanding of people and their surroundings. I have been designing websites, vector logos, icons, and animations since 2013 both as a hobby and in collaboration with other web developers. In 2019 I started designing and building websites for established local businesses on a freelance basis. I find myself constantly looking out for new trends and trying my hand at new skills. My current focus is back-end development. When I am not writing code I enjoy photography, cycling, kayaking, adventure travel and reading about new tech trends. Ultimately, I am passionate about making the web accessible to everyone.
